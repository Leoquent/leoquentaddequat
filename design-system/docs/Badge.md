Technical capability tag — 10px mono uppercase with translucent lime border. Used in wrap rows under card titles.

```tsx
<Badge>Workflow-Automation</Badge>
<Badge>CRM-Systeme</Badge>
<Badge variant="inverted">API-Vernetzung</Badge>
```

- `default` sits on dark surfaces; `inverted` (solid vanta, lime text) sits on lime surfaces (e.g. inside a hovered FeatureCard).
